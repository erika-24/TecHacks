Our Mission

Women make up over 50% of the population, but only earn 18% of computer science bachelor's degrees in the United States. TecHacks is an organization committed to creating a supportive environment for girls everywhere to create, problem-solve, and showcase their talents alongside like-minded females to compete and work with.
